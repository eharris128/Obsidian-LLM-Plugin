# Apollo Program

The Apollo program was the United States human spaceflight program that landed
the first humans on the Moon between 1969 and 1972.

## Missions

- Apollo 11 — first crewed lunar landing, July 1969
- Apollo 13 — "successful failure", crew returned safely after an oxygen tank
  explosion
- Apollo 17 — final lunar landing, December 1972
